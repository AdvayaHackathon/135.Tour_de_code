Top Tourist Places

KARNATAKA

"One State, Many Worlds"
- Capital: Bengaluru
-Famous For: IT industry, silk, ancient temples
-Major Cities: Mysuru, Mangaluru, Hubballi
-Culture & Festivals: Dasara, Ugadi, Hampi Utsav
- Tourist Attractions: Hampi, Coorg, Gokarna
Interesting Fact: Home to over 25 wildlife sanctuaries

GOA

"Pearl of the Orient"
Capital: Panaji
* Famous For: Beaches, Portuguese heritage, nightlife
Major Cities: Vasco da Gama, Margao, Mapusa
Culture & Festivals: Carnival, Shigmo, Christmas
Tourist Attractions: Baga Beach, Dudhsagar Falls, Basilica of Bom Jesus
Interesting Fact: Was a Portuguese colony until 1961

RAJASTHAN

"The Land of Kings"
Capital: Jaipur
* Famous For: Palaces, deserts, camel fairs
* Major Cities: Udaipur, Jodhpur, Bikaner
Culture & Festivals: Diwali, Gangaur, Desert Festival
Tourist Attractions: Thar Desert, Amber Fort, Mount Abu
Interesting Fact: Largest state in India by area.

MEGHALAYA

"Abode of Clouds"
Capital: Shillong
* Famous For: Rainfall, caves, living root bridges
* Major Cities: Shillong, Tura
Culture & Festivals: Wangala, Shad Suk Mynsiem
Tourist Attractions: Cherrapunji, Mawlynnong, Dawki River
Interesting Fact: One of the wettest places on Earth (Mawsynram)

JAMMU&KASHMIR

"Paradise on Earth"
Capital: Srinagar (Summer), Jammu (Winter)
Famous For: Snow-capped mountains, apples, saffron
Major Cities: Srinagar, Jammu, Anantnag
Culture & Festivals: Eid, Baisakhi, Lohri
Tourist Attractions: Dal Lake, Gulmarg, Vaishno Devi
Interesting Fact: Known for its beautiful handicrafts and shawls
